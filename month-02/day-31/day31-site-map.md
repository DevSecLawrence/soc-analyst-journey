## The Story My Portfolio Tells
 
A CS student in Nigeria who decided to teach himself SOC analysis from scratch, publicly, with no shortcuts. 30 days of daily labs, real investigations, and honest documentation. Not waiting to be ready — building in public while getting ready.
 
The portfolio needs to answer one question for a recruiter in under 30 seconds: **"Can this person actually do the work?"**
 
The answer is yes. The portfolio's job is to make that obvious.
